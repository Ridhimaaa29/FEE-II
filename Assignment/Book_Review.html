Think Like A Monk

book review

Author : Jay Shetty

Pubhlished in : 8 September 2020

Review :

"Think Like a Monk" by Jay Shetty is a spiritual self-help book that combines ancient wisdom with practical advice for modern living. Jay Shetty, a former monk turned social media star and motivational speaker, shares insights from his time in the monastery and how they can be applied to everyday life.
